AI-Driven Engineering Assistance System

(2023 - 2026)


Abstract

he objectives of the AIDEAS joint research project between the technological research institute Saint-Exupery and Grenoble Institute of Technology are twofold. First, it aims at fine tunning large language models for tailoring a conversational AI to systems engineering activities. Second, the objective is to define methodological guidelines for specifying, designing, and evaluating system engineer-conversational AI interaction.
