Bio

Dr. Pinquié is a research fellow at the Information Science and Systems Laboratory (LSIS UMR CNRS 7296), Arts & Métiers ParisTech engineering school, France. His research goal is methods and tools that enable companies to efficiently engineer complex socio-technical systems. His research combines aspects of design theory, systems engineering, modelling and simulation, data science, and information technology. He began as a R&T engineer in model-based systems engineering at the LISMMA research laboratory of SUPMECA engineering school, France. He has an MSc in Computational Engineering from the Cranfield University Applied Mathematics and Computing Group, a Ph.D in Product Design from Arts & Métiers ParisTech in 2016, and he was a post-doctoral research fellow in simulation-based systems engineering at Airbus Helicopters on behalf of LSIS UMR CNRS. He is a member of the French chapter of INCOSE (AFIS), the PLM Lab association, and the Design Society.
