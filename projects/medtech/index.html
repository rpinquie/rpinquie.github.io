MBSE for the Development of Drug-Device Combination Products

(01/01/2021 - 30/06/2024)


Abstract

[Context]This action research project will integrate various methodological engineering design elements into a model-based systems engineering framework (process, method, modelling method, modelling language, modelling software) to support the Advanced Drug Delivery Systems division of Becton Dickinson Medical Pharmaceutical Systems in the development of future new MedTech drug-device combination products.
