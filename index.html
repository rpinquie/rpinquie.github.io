Bio

Ph.D.-Ing. Romain Pinquié is Associate Professor at the School of Industrial Engineering of Grenoble Institute of technology (Grenoble INP) and a research fellow at the G-SCOP UMR CNRS 5272 - Laboratory of Design, Optimisation and Production. His research concentrates on design methods and tools for engineered systems by combining aspects of systems engineering, engineering design, modelling and simulation, virtual reality and data science.
